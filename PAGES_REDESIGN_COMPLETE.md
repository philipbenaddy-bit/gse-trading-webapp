## Design System Applied

### Color Palette
- **Primary:** Blue (#2563eb) - Finance industry standard
- **Success:** Emerald (#10b981) - Gains/positive
- **Danger:** Red (#ef4444) - Losses/negative
- **Accent:** Amber (#f59e0b) - Subtle premium touch
- **Neutrals:** 10-step gray scale

### Typography
- Clean sans-serif fonts
- Professional hierarchy
- Readable contrast ratios
- Tabular numbers for financial data

### Components
- **Cards:** White with subtle shadows
- **Buttons:** Solid colors, no gradients
- **Inputs:** Clean borders, blue focus rings
- **Icons:** Color-coded by feature

### Animations
- Subtle fade-in-up
- Smooth hover transitions
- Professional lift effects
- No excessive motion
