# Stock Ticker Implementation

## ✅ Continuous Scrolling Stock Ticker

A professional edge-to-edge stock ticker displaying live Ghana Stock Exchange prices at the top of every page.

## 🎨 Features

- ✅ **12 Ghana stocks** (MTN, GCB, SCB, GOIL, CAL, EBG, TOTAL, FML, SOGEGH, CLYD, AGA, UNIL)
- ✅ **Smooth infinite scroll** (60s loop, right to left)
- ✅ **Real-time updates** every 5 seconds
- ✅ **Color-coded trends** (green gains, red losses)
- ✅ **Hover to pause** functionality
- ✅ **Gradient edge fades** for seamless appearance
- ✅ **Dark theme** with professional styling

## 📁 Files

1. **`frontend/src/components/layout/StockTicker.tsx`** - New ticker component
2. **`frontend/src/components/layout/Layout.tsx`** - Added ticker to layout
